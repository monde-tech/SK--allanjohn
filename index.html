About the Candidate

A Leader Built for Service

I am Allan John "JONGSKIE" Mondejar, a proud resident of Barangay Cabancalan and a 4th-year Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering student at the University of Cebu Lapu-Lapu and Mandaue (UCLM).

As a CHED TDP-TES Government Scholar, I carry the responsibility of giving back to the community that has shaped me. My track record in academic excellence, organizational leadership, and grassroots community service speaks to my commitment to serve — not just with words, but with action.

My love for the youth of Cabancalan drives me. I believe every young person deserves programs that inspire, empower, and address their real needs. As your SK Councilor, that is exactly what I will deliver.
